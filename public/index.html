Our Royal Legacy

A Relentless Hunt for Flavor

Founded on the principle of the "Hunt"—a decade-long search across the subcontinent for the rarest spices and most authentic recipes of the Rajputana and Awadhi royalty.

At Spicy Hunt, we don't just cook—we archive history. Every dish served is a masterpiece of precision, using Mathania chillies, pure wild saffron, and techniques preserved for generations. We are not just a restaurant; we are the guardians of Indian culinary legacy.
